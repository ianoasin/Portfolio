About Me

As an IT student with a curious mind and a passion for innovation, I see technology not just as a field of study, but as a creative playground where ideas evolve into reality. Whether it’s writing clean code, troubleshooting systems, or exploring new technologies, I thrive on the challenge of turning problems into solutions that make an impact. From the first time I dissected a computer out of sheer curiosity, I knew this was more than just a career path — it was a calling. I’m constantly inspired by how technology connects people, drives change, and shapes the future. My academic journey is rooted in a strong foundation of programming, networking, and system analysis, but it’s the hands-on projects, late-night debugging marathons, and endless “aha!” moments that truly define who I am. Beyond the classroom, I’m always exploring — whether that means learning a new language (coding or spoken), contributing to team projects, or building something just for the fun of it. I believe in learning by doing, failing forward, and always staying curious. I’m not just preparing for the tech industry — I’m preparing to help shape it.
